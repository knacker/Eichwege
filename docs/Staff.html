Staff

Fabian Schuetze is the club owner. He was the captain of SG Burg for over 10 years and was elected as the new club owner of Blau-Weiss Eichwege in 2018.

Elias Kulke is the Head Coach of the Men's team. He is in this position since 2016 and was previously an active member in SV Doebern.

Niklas Albrecht is the Head Coach of the Women's team. He played for Blau-Weiss Eichwege since 1980. He retired as a player in 2004 and has the role of the coach since then.
